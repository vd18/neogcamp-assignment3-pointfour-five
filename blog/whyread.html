Why you should read charles bukowki's books?

march, 5

Charles Bukowski has been called an alcoholic, a narcissist, and a misogynist; but i dont think is care about what people call him. He is not the type who enamore with fancy words and high ideals Instead, he writes about harsh reality. While he uses his words in a very simple manner, they still manage to make a place in the reader’s heart

He writes about his real-life experiance and with that he also tell us about harsh reality that had been going on with him. he expose his ture toughts, emotion and how he felt in that situation throuh his books and poems. charles bukowski was brutally honest. in his first novel which was autobiography he talks about how he hated to go outside of the house, how his father often beat him to death. how much he hated most of all things that happan to him in his childhood and how he felt so powerless.

And other three books he talk about his life as teen, youth and his life after he get famous. his life was not full of drama, hustle, despair, happy,good,sad it was quite boring but how he write about it with his true thought and reality he makes his mark in readers heart
